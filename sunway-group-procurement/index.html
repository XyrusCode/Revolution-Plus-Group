Sunway Group Procurement

Sunway Group Procurement is to provide quality products and services in a timely manner to procurement customers and suppliers through variety of practices and tools which support the procurement overall mission. This include leveraging on eProcurement systems, value-added sourcing, sustainable procurement policies, processes and governance

Our working model is based on the combination of center-led and decentralized organisation structure approach.

Vision Statement
Transform Procurement into a value generator to the organization by positive contribution to the Group’s Growth and Profitability via Efficient Management of the Supply Chain based on our core values Integrity, Humility and Excellence.

Mission Statement
To provide professional support to all business units by :

  • Optimizing competitiveness through economies of scale
  • Establishing cost indices for competitiveness analysis
  • Procurement sourcing strategy to increase supplier base and new products/technology
  • Process, policy and skillset enhancement to increase efficiency & effectiveness
  • Leveraging on the technical expertise of the business units

Our key focus areas are:

  • Building materials
  • Information technology
  • Healthcare
  • Hospitality
  • Facility management
  • Other common products and services procured across all business units
