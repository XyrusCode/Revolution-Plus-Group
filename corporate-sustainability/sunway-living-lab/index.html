Sunway Living Lab

Sunway Living Lab aims to foster innovations, generate and incubate ideas to address the challenges facing humanity.

Our flagship smart sustainable city, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur, will be the test bed for Sunway Living Laboratory where we bring together researchers, experts and academia to generate real-world solutions for the prosperity and progress of the people and the planet.

Why Sunway City Kuala Lumpur?

Sprawling 800 acres, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur encompasses an enviable portfolio of more than 11,000 residential and commercial units, in addition to its multi-award-winning hospitality, retail, leisure, healthcare and education components.

Our township was built on a tin-mining wasteland and is a product of outstanding rehabilitation and restoration efforts. Regarded as a safe city, our township is a place where community can live, learn, work and play in a safe, healthy and connected environment for generations to come.

Talents

With the establishment of three universities and 42KL, a tuition-free programming school at Sunway FutureX that is located within the township, we continuously produce and groom talents towards ensuring smooth recruiting for various organisations and a seamless collaborative partnership with experts of different industries.

Liveability

As a smart sustainable township, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur features all the components of a liveable city: stability, healthcare, culture and environment, education and infrastructure. Convenience is key in our township: there are environmentally-friendly electric buses running on elevated tracks, as well as a covered canopy walkway with built-in solar panels connecting various facilities and amenities including an award-winning medical centre and universities within a 5km radius. In fact, the successful model of this township is replicated and remodelled in Sunway City Ipoh and Sunway City Iskandar Puteri.

Resources & Expertise

Home to Sunway Clinical Medical Centre, a collaborative effort with University of Cambridge, Jeffrey Sachs Center on Sustainable Development, Jeffrey Cheah Institute on Southeast Asia, Sunway Innovation Labs (iLabs), The Makerspace, three universities and Sunway’s 13 businesses, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur is the only place where one can easily tap into these resources under one roof. These establishments work closely with one another on academic research to incubate ideas and generate solutions.

Key Focus Areas

Our living lab initiatives focus on five key areas:

Smart Cities

Disrupting conventional real-estate markets through the use of artificial intelligence, blockchain and IoT to reimagine and transform the use of space. Through technology and new innovations, we aim to build sustainable cities which are smarter and healthier, while improving consumers’ convenience, connectivity and quality of life.

Super Accelerator
  • Sunway iLabs Super Accelerator is a four-month programme designed to provide funding and support to startups with at least an MVP or market-ready solution to accelerate their business in Sunway’s ecosystem. Each selected startup receives up to RM 100,000 in seed investment, mentorship and market access. We also facilitate engagement between startups and our corporate business divisions to ensure the smooth execution of innovative and impactful pilot projects at our designated test beds in Sunway City Kuala Lumpur.

Read more

Sunway Startup Immersion and Accelerator Programme
  • Sunway iLabs works closely with global partners to curate and design startup immersion and accelerator programmes to support the expansion of selected startups into the Southeast Asian market, using Malaysia as their main launchpad. Focusing on knowledge sharing, mentoring sessions and localised skills transfer, each programme enables startups to learn first-hand about the local startup ecosystem, industry landscape, business culture and regulations.
  • Our main objectives are to help global startups gain clarity and confidence, strategise with Sunway iLabs to offer the right product at the right time, and increase efficiency by developing localised products that meet customers’ needs at a lower cost within a shorter time.

  • Our first tripartite collaboration with Celcom and Huawei hopes to take the nation one step closer to achieving sustainable development via smart solutions, thus positioning Sunway City Kuala Lumpur as the launch pad of this ground-breaking partnership as one of Asia’s model smart sustainable cities.

Edutech

Complementing the education landscape through impact-driven teaching and learning tools to disrupt traditional education systems, by providing peer-to-peer, gamification, problem solving and hands-on learning on our campus.

Sunway FutureX Talent
  • We have collaborated with France-based 42, resulting in the setting up of 42KL, a coding school in Malaysia. It will be housed in Sunway FutureX building, as part of its FutureX Talent. 42 operates on a unique model that touts zero tuition fees, zero teachers and zero traditional classrooms. It offers peer-to-peer learning and is completely merit-based – the only application requirement is that students must be 18 years and above. Focusing on project-based learning imitating real-world work, the school encourages students to learn time management and build conceptual frameworks that can be applied to industry challenges and problem statements.

Digital Health Tech

Supporting healthcare organisations to enhance the efficiency of healthcare delivery through personalised, efficient and cutting-edge medical solutions. Harnessing the power of data and in-depth analysis, we are also looking to solve problems related to preventive health conditions such as non-communicable diseases (NCDs) and deep tech in healthcare.

Contactless COVID-19 Testing Pods
  • Sunway Medical Centre and Sunway iLabs have built a contactless COVID-19 testing pod, allowing healthcare workers in Malaysia to conduct testing for suspected cases in a safer and more efficient manner. The success of this project has been made possible due to the collaborative effort of various components and resources available at Sunway City Kuala Lumpur, a living lab with a thriving ecosystem where ideas are developed and turned into sustainable solutions.

Read more

Agri-Food Tech

Addressing challenges with tech-driven solutions throughout the farm-to-fork supply chain to create impact both upstream and downstream. The need to scale clean and healthy food production in Malaysia is vital, and we are looking to build decentralised smart urban farms throughout our cities across the country, with the goal of improving Malaysia’s national food security.

Sunway FutureX Farm
  • The first of its kind in Malaysia, FutureX Farm will be housed in the Sunway FutureX building at Sunway City Kuala Lumpur. It will serve as a skill-building hub for urban farming professionals, tech companies, researchers and young talents to collaborate and create transformative solutions focusing on food and agri-tech.
  • It focuses on the delivery of four key objectives – Nourish, Educate, Empower and Innovate. In partnership with Sunway Property, we aim to nourish our community with fresh produce grown at our farms and educate them about sustainable living and urban farming. We hope to sow the seeds of inspiration and, ultimately, empower homeowners to grow their own food. Bringing together a wide pool of expertise and resources from ecosystem players, Sunway FutureX aims to build decentralised urban farms to serve local communities, as well as to address supply chain challenges and the distance between farms to fork.

E-Commerce

Empowering businesses to adopt eCommerce and digital solutions for faster growth, to reach new markets and to explore additional revenue streams. Together with our partners, we aim to build a holistic ecosystem by providing innovative solutions such as O2O enablers, logistics, omni-channel marketing, payment and security.

Alibaba Global E-commerce Talent (GET) Programme
  • Sunway iLabs plays a role in mentoring the students on their e-commerce start-up projects and connecting them to the ecosystem.
eCommerce Jumpstart Programme
  • The eCommerce Jumpstart Programme aims to help retail SMEs and entrepreneurs on their digital transformation journey to weather through the economic downturn we are facing using a structured curriculum and mentorship with digital experts – at no charge. It is a free four-week training programme for SMEs and retail entrepreneurs to help their businesses get online. It is hosted by Sunway Innovation Labs (iLabs) and Sunway University in partnership with numerous industry leaders, government agencies and digital champions.
