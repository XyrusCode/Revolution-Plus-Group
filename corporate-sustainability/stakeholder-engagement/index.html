Our Stakeholders' Material Concerns

In 2019, we conducted an in-depth stakeholder engagement and materiality assessment exercise. Here are the top 10 material topics, based on the survey recorded from our stakeholders.

WATER SECURITY

GRI: Water
Risks

Disruptions to businesses and operations

Opportunities

Leverage Build-Own-Operate business model to increase community resilience

Impact & Boundaries

Customers, builders and suppliers, government and regulators, investors, community and media

Long-term Management Approach
  • Identification of high-risk areas and building redundancy
  • All landscape to be irrigated by recycled supply by 2030
  • 50% of water supply in commercial buildings located in Sunway City Kuala Lumpur to be sourced from recycled supply by 2030
  • Develop new solutions with our young entrepreneurs and to test them in our cities/developments

WATER POLLUTION

GRI: Water, Waste and Effluent
Risks
  • Health risks to humans and biodiversity, affecting social license to operate
  • Fines that affect finances and reputation
Opportunities
  • Compost food waste for community use or urban farming to build resilience
  • Partnerships with others who use waste streams as input
Impact & Boundaries

All stakeholders across our value chain

Long-term Management Approach
  • Stormwater management, silt trap and particle monitoring for all developments as a standard practice
  • Waste management policies
  • Urban farming to develop entrepreneurs, build community resilience and reduce urban heat

AIR POLLUTION (IMPACTING HEALTH)

GRI: Emissions, Occupational Health and Safety, Customer Health and Safety
Risks
  • Health risks to humans and biodiversity, affecting social license to operate
  • Fines that affect finances and reputation
Opportunities

Develop competitive advantage in property products with homes catering towards well-being

Impact & Boundaries

All stakeholders across our value chain

Long-term Management Approach
  • Total Suspended Particles (TSP) measurement for all worksites
  • Built-in well-being features in all new property products

CUSTOMER HEALTH AND SAFETY

GRI: Customer Health and Safety
Risks

Business and reputational risks

Opportunities

New solutions for township and development security as competitive advantage

Impact & Boundaries

Customers, community, government and regulators and media

Long-term Management Approach

Test-bedding scalable and replicable new security solutions at township levels

OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY

GRI: Occupational Health and Safety
Risks
  • Safety and health of workers affect productivity
  • Fines that affect finances and reputation
Opportunities

Partnerships with leading authorities and academics to pilot industry-leading standards

Impact & Boundaries

Employees, government and regulators and media

Long-term Management Approach

Build track record as a competitive advantage for construction division

CUSTOMER PRIVACY

GRI: Customer Privacy
Risks

Cyberattacks can harm business and erode trust

Opportunities

Develop talent and test-bed new solutions

Impact & Boundaries

Employees, government and regulators and media

Long-term Management Approach

Develop new talent in Sunway University and test-bed new solutions that can be implemented or commercialised

ENERGY SECURITY

GRI: Energy, Emissions
Risks

Electricity tariff hikes increase operational costs

Opportunities

Develop competitive advantage in the construction division and implement it in developments to save energy for managed assets and as a competitive advantage for build-to-sell products

Impact & Boundaries

Customers, builders and suppliers, government and regulators, investors, community and media

Long-term Management Approach
  • Develop competitive advantage in construction division
  • Increase renewable energy in energy mix to save operational costs and implement it in new products as competitive advantage

AIR POLLUTION (GLOBAL WARMING)

GRI: Emissions
Risks
  • Health risks to surrounding communities, affecting social license to operate
  • Fines that affect finances and reputation
Opportunities

Leverage on improving the public network transportation system in Malaysia to deliver transit-linked development as competitive advantage

Impact & Boundaries

All stakeholders across our value chain

Long-term Management Approach
  • Landbank acquisition strategy to include consideration for emissions
  • 40% waste diverted from landfill by 2030
  • Test-bed replicable and scalable solutions in our cities

BIODIVERSITY (ECOSYSTEM POLLUTION)

GRI: Biodiversity
Risks
  • Health risks to surrounding communities, affecting social license to operate
  • Fines that affect finances and reputation
Opportunities

Build track record and capabilities as a sustainable development leader with governance for pollution management through value chain

Impact & Boundaries

Builders and suppliers, community and the media

Long-term Management Approach

Implement policies and create systems for ecosystem pollution management

CLIMATE CHANGE

GRI: Emissions
Risks

Damage to assets, environment and society

Opportunities

All stakeholders across our value chain

  • Construction division to develop Virtual Design Construction models that take into account climate resilience
  • Develop urban farms to build robustness
  • Built-in climate resilience in managed assets and build-to-sell-assets
