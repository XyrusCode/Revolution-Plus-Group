Leadership & Expertise
Sunway VCP Sdn Bhd is originally established in 1991 as a joint venture between Sunway Holdings Incorporated Berhad and Keramo Steinzeug N.V. of Belgium. Both partners have proven, long established track records in glazed vitrified clay pipes (vcp) in their respective markets.
This joint venture combines the expertise of one of Europe's leaders in clay pipe technology with one of the largest and most diversified conglomerates in Malaysia.

Sunway VCP is a member of the Sunway Group of Companies, a diversified conglomerate with extensive interests in quarrying, construction, industrial products and property development.

Sunway VCP is playing a vital role in Malaysia by making available vcp of the highest quality, which are raising sewerage and drainage standards throughout the country, as it modernizes towards the vision of 2020.
