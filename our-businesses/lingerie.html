Angelic Lingerie is the trademarked arm of the RevolutionPlus Group birthed from the desire to provide quality and affordable undergarment. The brand stands on the mandate to design comfortable, classy and stylish underwear for both male and female genders. The brand caters for all classes of consumers be it high end or just classy seekers

Angelic Lingerie has its own registered trademark with customized designs and production line from the United State of America. The brand also sells other high end designers lingerie and labels for customers who have specific preferences. Angelic Lingerie is situated on the Island with plans to expand outlets across Nigeria.
