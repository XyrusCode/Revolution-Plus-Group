Trading and Manufacturing

A Leading Distributor with an Extensive Regional Network

The Trading & Manufacturing division operates through five major product groups, namely Hoses and Fittings, Heavy Equipment, Heavy Equipment Parts, Building Materials, and Industrial Hardware.  We serve a diverse range of industries including agriculture, construction, logging, manufacturing, marine, mining, oil and gas, as well as quarrying.

Our success is largely attributed to our quality products and in-house brands, such as Sunflex and TotalRubber hoses and fittings, Suntrak heavy equipment parts, Sunlube20 lubricants and the Nietz and Picasaf range of hardware products. Aligned with our commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, we have integrated sustainability principles into our operations with quality management processes as a key priority.

Heavy Equipment
Learn more

Sunway Enterprise (1988) Sdn. Bhd. is a leading distributor of premier quarrying, construction and material-handling equipment. We are one of the key business units of the Trading and Manufacturing Division of Sunway, serving a diverse range of industries including quarrying, construction, marine, oil and gas, mining, agriculture, manufacturing, logistics, warehousing and logging. Our 3S branch network is strategically located across Malaysia to support our customers.

Our success is largely attributed to the trusted reputation and excellence of our world-renowned brands, known for their quality, performance and value. Our staff are well trained, dedicated and committed to providing excellent after-sales service and supporting our customers.

In addition, our team of well-trained and experienced technicians is able to take on any major refurbishment of Furukawa hydraulic crawler drills and Sany concrete pumps to factory standards. With a computerised-part inventory system, we are able to provide prompt after-sales service and parts support to serve our customers better.

Our vision is to identify and introduce new products to the market, all while maintaining existing product agencies and expanding their market share through new product and market development. We are constantly working towards becoming the region’s most successful and preferred supplier of quarry, construction and material-handling equipment.

Hoses and Fittings
Learn more

Sunway Hoses and Fittings Division is a one-stop centre for hoses, fittings, couplings, tubes and accessories, as well as a service provider. As one of the largest stockists of hoses, coupling, adaptors and fittings in Asia Pacific, we possess large operation and warehouse facilities located in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, China, Indonesia, Vietnam and Australia to facilitate our customer’s needs.

Our Hoses and Fittings Division carries our own SUNFLEX brand, which has a proven track record for quality. We also offer both excellent pre- and post-sales services.

We leverage all of the above to successfully differentiate ourselves from our competitors and serve our many customers in construction, offshore, marine, oil and gas, and the shipyard sectors, as well as transportation, mining and logging.

We are also ISO 9001:2015-certified and a member of NAHAD (Association for Hoses and Accessories Distribution).

Sunway Winstar Sdn. Bhd. was established in 1980 and became a member of Sunway Group in 2015.

We are one of the largest trading companies for hardware products in Malaysia, specialising in supplying a wide range of products such as industrial hardware, DIY hardware, personal safety products, lubricants, epoxy adhesive, car care products, welding, household, aluminium, building materials and more.

Heavy Equipment Parts
Learn more

Established in 1983, Sunway Marketing Heavy Equipment Parts (HEP) Division is primarily involved in the trading and distribution of aftermarket parts for heavy equipment, combine harvesters as well as on-road and off-road trucks. We serve a global customer base of dealership networks and end-users in diverse industries ranging from construction, mining, quarry, logging, plantation, agriculture, marine, logistics and transportation and more.

Our main product categories include undercarriage parts, ground engaging tools, engine parts, lubricants and greases, and truck parts such as Truck Bus Radial (TBR) tyres, bias tyres, Four-Wheel Drive tyres, wheel rims, engine oils, filters and more. We have established our presence and network in China and throughout ASEAN countries including Malaysia, Thailand and Indonesia.
