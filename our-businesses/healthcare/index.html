Healthcare

Malaysia’s Top Healthcare Provider

Sunway Healthcare Group (SHG) is a leading integrated private healthcare group with a network of hospitals and health services in Malaysia. Following the success of Sunway Medical Centre, we opened our second tertiary hospital, Sunway Medical Centre Velocity in September 2019. Together, the two hospitals have a combined capacity of 800 beds and close to 300 consultant specialists.

As part of our expansion strategy to provide more communities with access to quality healthcare, we will increase our footprint all over Malaysia with a hospital in Seberang Jaya (Penang), Sunway Damansara (Selangor), Iskandar Puteri (Johor), Ipoh (Perak), Kota Bahru (Kelantan) and Paya Terubong (Penang) respectively. This growth will propel us to be one of the largest healthcare groups in the region, with a combined capacity of over 3,500 beds throughout our network of hospitals. Pledging our support for the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDG), we proactively support the nation’s agenda in raising healthcare standards in Malaysia through reaching out and educating the community on health issues and the importance of healthcare, which is also in line with the vision of the Ministry of Health to achieve better health. We also work in partnership with numerous NGOs to ensure equitable and essential health services for all.

Our primary focus is to deliver excellent medical outcome through patient-centric care. We continue to improve the lives we touch through holistic healthcare offerings such as ambulatory care, traditional and complementary medicine, home care services, as well as health-screening and GP services. Our reputation is well known in the region as our hospitals have received more than 43,000 international patients from over 135 countries.

With the aim to shape the future of healthcare with world-class healthcare professionals, we seek to enhance collaboration in the domain of medicine and medical education. Together with Sunway Education Group, collaborations with world-class institutions such as University of Cambridge, Royal College of Physicians (London), Royal Papworth Hospital and Harvard Medical School have been established for continuous development of our healthcare professionals. The partnership with University of Cambridge, in particular, also includes recognising our Clinical Research Centre as the sole Regional Site Partner to conduct and coordinate research involving human patients.

Sunway Medical Centre @ Sunway City Kuala Lumpur
Learn more

Established in November 1999, Sunway Medical Centre is an Australian Council on Healthcare Standards (ACHS) accredited private hospital located in Malaysia’s first fully integrated green township of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur.

We currently offer comprehensive tertiary healthcare services, with a total of 617 beds, more than 200 consultation suites, 12 operating theatres and supported by two multi-storey car parks with a total of 1,470 parking bays. The cutting-edge technology available in Sunway Medical Centre includes, the Varian TrueBeam STx, da Vinci Robotics Surgical System, Elekta Leksell Gamma Knife Icon, Zeiss Intrabeam IORT, Siemens xSPECT-CT, Siemens Somatom Force Dual Source CT scanner and Radixact-X9 Tomotherapy. We also implement multiple sustainability initiatives in line with the UN-SDG such as heightening public awareness of the importance of healthcare through free health screenings, public forums and medical symposia, as well as our Nursing Scholarship programme.

Given the growing medical tourism industry, we are embarking on an extensive expansion plan next to the existing hospital building to better cater to the international market. Tower C commenced its first phase of operations in October 2016 with the new Cancer and Radiosurgery Centre and Nuclear Medicine Centre. It also houses other Centres of Excellence such as Digestive Health Centre, Bone and Joint Centre and the expanded Haemodialysis Centre. The second phase, with a 10-level tower, that houses all single-room wards and consultation suites, started its operations in 2017. This sets Sunway Medical Centre as one of the leading private medical centres in the region.

Sunway Medical Centre has also won the Orthopaedic Service Provider of the Year Award at the GHT-Bernstein 2017 APAC Healthcare & Medical Tourism Awards, which recognises healthcare providers that have maintained consistently high standards in delivering customer value and demonstrated outstanding performances in initiatives that improve the way healthcare is delivered.

Sunway Medical Centre Velocity is a comprehensive tertiary-care hospital in the township of Sunway Velocity, Cheras. Strategically located in an integrated commercial and residential development nearby Velocity Mall and Velocity Hotel, the hospital is less than 4km away from Kuala Lumpur City Centre.

We offer comprehensive tertiary healthcare services with designed capacity of 240 beds, 77 consultant suites and six operating theatres. We also offer advanced medical technologies and state-of-the-art facilities for outpatient and inpatient speciality care, health and wellness programmes, not forgetting our 24-hour emergency services.

We provide patients with the best care, from the initial consultation and diagnosis, through to surgery or treatment and then follow-up care and rehabilitation. Our team of highly experienced healthcare professionals will ensure that your journey here is well coordinated.

We are also highly involved in CSR, initiating health programmes consisting of CPR-AED workshops and behavioural health talks. These programmes, in line with the UN-SDG, are tailored for the public to educate them on the importance of healthcare – both from a physical and mental aspect.

Sunway Home Healthcare
Learn more

Sunway Home Healthcare is a unique hospital-based home-care structure, and an organised care-at-home facility affiliated to the group of hospitals and centres of Sunway Healthcare Group.

We were established in 2003 with a lean structure of a pure nursing care provider to cater to the unique sphere of services for patients at home. As the need of home healthcare increases due to various factors such as cost, time, convenience, benefits and outcome, we grew to become a multi-disciplinary care provider.

We provide patients’ total needs from simple wound or post-natal care to physiotherapy, dietetics, medical and even palliative care that are performed by skilled and experienced healthcare professionals.

Our certified, experienced and trustworthy personnel provide a wide range of in-home health services to give you peace of mind as we keep your loved ones safe while living at home.
