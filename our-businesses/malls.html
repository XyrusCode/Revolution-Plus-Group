Malls

Among Malaysia’s Largest Mall Owner-Operators

The mall division owns and manages a portfolio of leading retail malls across Malaysia, strategically located in master-planned townships or mixed developments. We manage seven retail malls with a combined net lettable area (NLA), of existing projects and those in the pipeline, to an excess of 7.7 million sq. ft. They include our flagship retail property Sunway Pyramid in Sunway City Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Velocity Mall, Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Big Box Retail Park, Sunway Iskandar Puteri Johor; Sunway Citrine, Sunway Iskandar Puteri, Johor; Sunway Putra Mall, Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Giza, Sunway Damansara; and Sunway Carnival Shopping Mall, Penang.

Sunway Malls is on track to become Malaysia’s largest shopping-mall owner and operator with five more assets in the pipeline, and targets to increase its NLA to 10.2 million square feet in the near future.

Sunway Pyramid
Learn more

First opened its doors in 1997, Sunway Pyramid is Malaysia’s first themed shopping and entertainment mall. It has undergone a magnificent transformation to herald a new shopping kingdom, boasting 4.3 million sq. ft. of retail space. With an Egyptian-inspired pyramid and a monumental lion standing guard at its entrance, our mall features four shopping precincts – Fashion Central, Oasis Boulevard, Asian Avenue and Marrakesh, offering a variety of specialty outlets.

Easily accessible via an air-conditioned walkway on the First Floor, Sunway Pyramid West boasts an area of 100,000 sq. ft., with a plethora of family-friendly F&B and retail offerings as well as an all-in-one child enrichment and edutainment centre.

Sunway Giza Mall
Learn more

Opened since 2009, Sunway Giza Mall is a 160,000 sq. ft., three-storey mall with a covered boulevard. Our 6.4-acre specialty retail centre comprises a vivacious mix of 48 units of shop offices and a three-storey lifestyle neighbourhood mall with the convenience of an 800-bay basement carpark.

As Kota Damansara’s friendly neighbourhood mall, we employ environmentally friendly practices such as using “picture framed” openable windows to maximise day light and natural ventilation, as well as incorporating energy-efficient T5 lighting with colourful illumination, adding to the mall’s design and architecture.

Sunway Carnival Mall
Learn more

With 500,000 sq. ft. of net lettable area (NLA) featuring 220 shops and a convention centre, as well as the region’s largest cinema with an eight-screen cineplex and an amusement centre, Sunway Carnival Mall is Penang’s must-visit retail haven. Situated near the Penang Bridge, we are accessible via the North-South Highway and Butterworth-Kulim Expressway.

Currently on the next phase of expansion, its gross floor area (GFA) will double from the current 780,000 sq. ft. to 1.5 million sq. ft. The expansion phase will add an additional 500,000 square feet of new NLA, due for completion in the fourth quarter of 2020.
